About me 👋

Hello! I’m a Mechatronics and Robotics Engineering student at Queen’s University with a deep passion for advancing the medical robotics industry. My journey in engineering has been driven by
a desire to blend technology and healthcare to create a positive, tangible impact on people’s lives. 🌍
I'm fascinated by robotics, control systems, and machine learning, and I aim to leverage these fields to develop innovative solutions that revolutionize healthcare. By working on medical robotics, I hope
to enhance patient well-being and support healthcare professionals in delivering high-quality care
through cutting-edge technology. 🩺
I’m committed to pushing the boundaries of what’s possible, exploring new ways to make a meaningful difference in the future of healthcare. Whether it's through developing intelligent
surgical robots, creating advanced diagnostic tools, or building systems that empower doctors and
patients alike, I believe that technology has an incredible role to play in transforming healthcare
for the better. 🌱
If you're interested in healthcare, robotics, AI, or just want to connect and discuss ideas, feel free to reach out! I’m always open to new conversations, collaborations, and learning opportunities.
